# Conversion tracking setup

**Required in Google Tag Manager BEFORE launching ads:**

1. **GA4 conversion event:** `form_submit_lp`
   - Trigger: form submission on any `/kw/*` page
   - Parameters: `lp_slug`, `form_type`, `budget_range`
2. **Google Ads conversion import** from GA4
3. **Conversion value:** ₹5,000 per submission (lead value)
4. **Counting:** One per click (not all)
5. **Window:** 30-day click, 1-day view
6. **Inclusion in "Conversions":** Yes

**Verify before launch:** Submit a test form on each LP, confirm conversion fires in GA4 Real-Time + Google Ads conversion preview tool.

# Critical notes

1. **Conversion tracking is non-negotiable.** Smart bidding fails without it. Don't launch ads until form submissions fire as conversions in GA4 + Google Ads.
2. **Don't touch bids in week 1.** Smart bidding needs 7–14 days of learning data. Manual interference resets the learning phase.
3. **Quality Score depends on H1 = ad headline = keyword match.** That's why each ad group has its own LP. Don't merge them.
4. **Negatives are the difference between 5x and 0.5x ROAS.** Review search terms daily for first 2 weeks.
5. **Lead response time matters more than ad copy.** A lead that waits 24 hours for response converts 60% less than one called in 5 minutes. Sales follow-up SLA must be < 30 minutes.
