# Mailchimp Sequences

## Web Development Leads

Template source:

- `web-development-sequence.ts`

Use this sequence only for leads from:

- Route: `/web-development-company`
- Form types: `web-dev`, `web-dev-premium`
- Mailchimp tag: `web-development-lead`

Recommended Mailchimp merge fields:

- `FNAME`
- `EMAIL`
- `PROJECT_TYPE`
- `BUDGET`
- `PHONE`
- `SOURCE_PAGE`
- `LAST_CTA`

Recommended automation rules:

- Start when the contact receives tag `web-development-lead`.
- Stop when the contact replies, books a call, becomes qualified in CRM, or is manually tagged `sales-qualified`.
- Do not use this sequence for AI, mobile, ecommerce, enterprise, or custom software leads unless the user came from the web development page.

Sequence timing:

- Email 1: immediate
- Email 2: day 2
- Email 3: day 4
- Email 4: day 6
- Email 5: day 8
- Email 6: day 10
- Email 7: day 12
- Email 8: day 14
- Email 9: day 16
- Email 10: day 18

Main nurture angles:

- Website is a marketing and sales tool, not a brochure.
- AI integration matters when it reduces buyer friction and manual follow-up.
- Test the developer with a small scope before giving the full project.
- Strategy comes before design.
- Stack choice should fit the business model.
- Cheap websites become expensive when they block tracking, ownership, speed, or follow-up.

CTA style:

- Keep every email consultative, not pushy.
- End with one clear action: send the current website, idea, or competitor reference.
- Promise a useful reply: the first three decisions, what to build, what to avoid, and the fastest safe next step.
- Include both WhatsApp CTA and email-reply fallback.

Primary CTA:

- WhatsApp: `https://wa.me/919818565561?text=Hi%20RDMI%2C%20I%20want%20to%20discuss%20my%20web%20development%20project.`

- Email 2: day 2

- Email 5: day 8
- Email 6: day 10

Main nurture angles:

- Diagnose the business workflow before writing a feature list.
- Decide what to build, buy, automate, or avoid.

- Test the software partner with a discovery sprint, prototype, or small production module first.

Main nurture angles:

- Add AI with clear guardrails, permissions, source references, and human approval.
